— The 4 Zones —

One Session.
Four Worlds.

Every Gyaan Garage session takes students through 4 uniquely designed zones. Each one is a completely different science experience.

⚡
ZONE 01
The Wow Room
Instructor-led spectacle experiments that make your jaw drop. This is science as theatre — dramatic, loud, and unforgettable.
🔧
ZONE 02
The Build Lab
Students build something real — a telescope, periscope, pinhole camera — and take it home. Every student leaves with proof they made something.
🏆
ZONE 03
The Challenge Floor
Science as competition. Teams battle it out on timed challenges. The floor is loud, fast, and full of winning moments.
🔍
ZONE 04
The Mystery Room
One student. One problem. Zero instructions. This is the hardest zone — and the one students talk about the most. Figure it out yourself.
